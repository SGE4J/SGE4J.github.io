SmartBudget

The SmartBudget mobile application will help the user to organize his or her money. This is a daily tool that organizes and plans ahead expenses and future payments for the user. In addition, this application makes the userâ€™s life easy because it is a handy tool that people can easily download on their mobile devices to keep track of every monetary movement.

Smart Budget Objective

  • To keep the application simple and user friendly, so people could easily understand and use all the features in the application.
  • To help people manage their spending, so they can keep track of their expenses and income entrances.
  • Gives alert when the user approaches a certain budget of a certain spending category.
  • Gives the user a freedom to divide their spending categories by percentage.
  • Help users strategize their next cycle of spending when they failed to meet their budget in the previous cycle.
  • Gives statistics of spending categories between cycles.
